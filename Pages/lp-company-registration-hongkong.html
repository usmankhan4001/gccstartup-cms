Why Hong Kong in the first place?

Benefits of Company Registration in Hong Kong for Dutch Founders.

01

0% Corporate Tax on Foreign Income

All revenue earned from clients outside Hong Kong is taxed at 0% corporate rate — this is Hong Kong's territorial tax law, not a loophole. A Dutch entrepreneur earning €100,000/year from international clients pays 0% corporate tax through a Hong Kong entity.

02

No Personal or Wealth Taxes

No personal income tax in Hong Kong for non-residents — profits retained inside the company are not taxed at the personal level in HK. No wealth tax, no capital gains tax, no Box 3 equivalent — income stays intact inside the company.

03

Global Trust & Platforms

Hong Kong has been a globally trusted financial jurisdiction for over 50 years, accepted by Stripe, Amazon, PayPal, and all major platforms. No offshore stigma.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Hong Kong registration.

Do I need to visit Hong Kong to open a bank account? +
No. We partner with digital banking platforms like Airwallex and Wise, which allow for a 100% remote account opening process.
How does the 0% territorial tax system work? +
Hong Kong only taxes profits derived from a trade, profession, or business carried out in Hong Kong. If your business operations, clients, and suppliers are located outside of Hong Kong, you can apply for offshore tax exemption and pay 0% corporate tax.
Does Hong Kong require a local director? +
No local director is required — you can be the sole director and shareholder. A local company secretary and registered address are required, both of which we provide.
Is an audit required every year? +
Yes, Hong Kong companies must file an annual audited return. It is straightforward for small companies and we can arrange the audit and filing for you.
