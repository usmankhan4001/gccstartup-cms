Global Company Structures, Built for Founders Who Move Fast
Launch, bank and structure your international business with a compliance-first partner across the UAE, Bahrain, Hong Kong, Singapore and Ireland.
CTA: Speak to a Structuring Advisor
Website tone: premium, direct and compliance-led. Use the figures below as baseline marketing facts and verify final legal/tax wording before publishing.
United Arab Emirates (UAE)
Gulf hub for companies that need regional credibility, banking access and investor/residency pathways.
Tax baseline
9% Corporate Tax / 0% on Foreign Income / 0% Personal Income Tax
Setup timeline
Approx. 30 days, including required in-person huddle / medical visit
Starting structural fee
From $1,500 base government fee
Company Registration & Setup
Launch your United Arab Emirates (UAE) company through a clean, end-to-end formation path designed for founders who need speed, credibility and compliant execution. GCC Startup coordinates the name reservation, approvals, license filing and local statutory steps so your business can move from idea to operating structure with confidence.
Typical requirements: Passport copies, proof of address, proposed company names and business activity details.
Global & Local Bank Account Setup
Build the financial rails your company needs from day one. We prepare the corporate profile, ownership story and business model documentation for local banks and trusted fintech platforms, helping your account application look clear, credible and investor-ready.
Typical requirements: Corporate documents, UBO/KYC records, shareholder background profile and bank reference or statement history where required.
Nominee UBO - Tier 02 Privacy Package
Protect public-facing exposure while staying aligned with compliance expectations. Our nominee privacy structure is supported by due diligence, legal declarations and control documentation so operational rights remain clearly protected.
Typical requirements: KYC clearance, beneficiary identity records and signed trust/authorization documentation.
Personal Tax Residency & Visas
Create a personal footprint around your company with practical residency, tax-positioning and substance guidance. We support document preparation, local appointments and compliance checkpoints so your structure is more than a registration on paper.
Typical requirements: Passport or entry records, biometrics/medical checks where applicable, local substance records and tax-residency evaluation files.
